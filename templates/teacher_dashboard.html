How EDAPTIV Works

When you upload study materials, EDAPTIV's AI automatically adapts them for each student based on their learning style (visual, auditory, kinesthetic, or reading/writing) and any learning challenges they have (dyslexia, ADHD, autism, etc.). This ensures every student receives content optimized for their unique needs.
